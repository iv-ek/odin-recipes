Peanut Butter Brownies

Description:

Chocolate and peanut butter come together in these decadent, fudgy treats.

Ingredients:

  • 90 g dairy free chocolate chips
  • 73 g vegan butter OR coconut oil
  • 22 g dutch processed cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 200 g granulated sugar
  • 118 ml soy milk
  • 125 g all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 90 g additional chocolate chips, optional

Peanut Butter Swirl

  • 129 g creamy peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon vegan butter, melted
  • 40 g powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
