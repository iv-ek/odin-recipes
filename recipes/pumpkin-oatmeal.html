Pumpkin Oatmeal

Description:

It’s a quick, easy, and healthy breakfast made with 7 ingredients, like real pumpkin and pumpkin pie spice

Ingredients:

  • 81 g rolled oats
  • 123 g pumpkin puree
  • 355 ml soy milk
  • 2 g pumpkin pie spice
  • 2 ml pure vanilla extract
  • pinch of salt
  • 12 g brown sugar
  • Toppings chopped walnuts or pecans, more sweetener, more milk, yogurt or whipped topping

Steps:

  1. Add the oats, pumpkin, milk, pumpkin pie spice, pinch of salt and sweetener of choice to a small pot.
  2. Bring to a simmer over medium heat, and cook, stirring frequently, for about 3-4 minutes, until the oats are softened.
  3. Serve in bowls topped with additional sweetener, nuts, milk, whipped topping or yogurt. Enjoy!

Notes:

  1. The leftovers will keep for 3-4 days.
  2. For overnight oats, add all ingredients to a bowl with a lid and stir well to combine. Let it sit in the fridge overnight and up to 4 days.
  3. If you don’t have pumpkin pie spice mix, you can use the following spice mix instead: 3/4 teaspoon cinnamon, 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger, 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg.
